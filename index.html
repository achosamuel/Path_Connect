Watch the video to learn more about Path Connect

Path Connect is the dream and project of two students who understand the challenges young people face when planning their future. As students, we’ve encountered the same struggles—uncertainty, limited resources, and the feeling of being stuck without the right information..

We believe that information is power. Without it, dreams can’t take shape. That’s why we created Path Connect: a platform dedicated to bridging the gap between students and the opportunities that can transform their lives.
